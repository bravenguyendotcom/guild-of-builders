One game. Four generations. The Builder grows with the game.

| Vol | Role | The child can say... | Pro-ladder rung рҹҸ—пёҸ |
|-----|------|----------------------|---------------------|
| **I** | The First Voyage | "I built a game." | **Vocabulary** вҖ” the trade has names |

# рҹ“ҳ VOLUME I вҖ” The First Voyage

**Rung:** vocabulary вҖ” every skill quietly gets its real-world name.
*(Chapters map to the 20-mission ladder in `CURRICULUM.md`.)*

# рҹ“— VOLUME II вҖ” Growing the Adventure
**Python:** data structures in depth, files, modules, `try`/`except`. **Deliverable:** *Treasure Quest v2* (same game, evolved).
**Rung:** rituals вҖ” plan before you build; iterate in small steps.

**Ch 1 вҖ” The Map Grows** вҖ” Story: the crew dreams bigger; Dragon Debug says *plan first.*
Learn: writing a tiny spec. Game: the v2 wishlist. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *spec / requirements.*

**Ch 2 вҖ” The Knapsack** вҖ” Story: too much loot, no pockets. Learn: lists & dicts in depth.
Game: a real inventory system. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *state.*

**Ch 3 вҖ” The Merchant's Wharf** вҖ” Story: a shopkeeper who haggles. Learn: functions that
return values, nested data. Game: buying & selling; first true NPC. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *interface.*

**Ch 4 вҖ” When Things Go Wrong** вҖ” Story: Captain Byte feeds the ship nonsense and it sinks.
Learn: `try`/`except`, graceful failure. Game: the game stops crashing on bad input. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *exception / edge case.*

**Ch 5 вҖ” The Memory Stone** вҖ” Story: a stone that remembers your journey. Learn: reading &
writing files (save/load). Game: **save and continue.** рҹҸ—пёҸ: *persistence.* Cast: Quackers (check it saved).

**Ch 6 вҖ” The Cartographer** вҖ” Story: a world with many rooms. Learn: data-driven maps
(dicts of rooms). Game: a multi-location world. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *data-driven design.* Cast: **Ninja Cat** (navigation).

**Ch 7 вҖ” The Toolmakers' Guild** вҖ” Story: one giant scroll is impossible to carry. Learn:
modules & `import`; split the code. Game: the game becomes many tidy files. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *modules / codebase.*

**Ch 8 вҖ” The Bestiary** вҖ” Story: monsters with real personalities. Learn: weighted randomness,
data-driven creatures. Game: richer, fairer encounters. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *configuration.* Cast: Bug Monster evolves.

**Ch 9 вҖ” The Riddle Theatre Returns** рҹҺӯ вҖ” Story: Monty & Quizzalot upgrade their act. Learn:
dictionaries of riddles/dialogue. Game: puzzle NPCs. Cast: **Monty & Quizzalot.**

**Ch 10 вҖ” The Standup** вҖ” Story: a daily two-minute check-in with the Captain. Learn: tracking
a tiny backlog, working in iterations. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *standup / backlog / iteration.*

**Ch 11 вҖ” Pycasso's Palette** рҹҺЁ вҖ” Story: color comes to the terminal. Learn: ANSI color,
formatted tables, simple art. Game: a handsome text UI. Cast: **Pycasso.**

**Ch 12 вҖ” Treasure Quest v2** *(capstone / donut)* вҖ” the **same** game, grown up. Achievement: **World Builder** рҹ—әпёҸ.

# рҹ“ҷ VOLUME III вҖ” The Living World
**Python:** OOP, architecture, event-driven UI, assets, packaging, publishing (web-first).
**Deliverable:** *Treasure Quest v3* вҖ” a **published** game with a shareable URL.
**Rung:** artifacts вҖ” a README strangers can use; a demo you present with confidence.

**Ch 1 вҖ” The Blueprint** вҖ” Story: the ship's too big to hold in your head. Learn: thinking in
objects (nouns of the game). рҹҸ—пёҸ: *architecture.*

**Ch 2 вҖ” Meet the Class** вҖ” Story: Player, Monster, Item become *things* with their own powers.
Learn: classes & objects. Game: entities refactored into classes. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *class / object.* Cast: Dragon Debug (design).

**Ch 3 вҖ” Inheritance Island** вҖ” Story: a family of monsters. Learn: inheritance & polymorphism.
Game: a monster hierarchy. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *reuse.*

**Ch 4 вҖ” The Stage Appears** вҖ” Story: the game gets a *screen.* Learn: GUI/web, event-driven
programming (clicks, input). Game: the first real window. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *event loop / UI.*

**Ch 5 вҖ” Pycasso's Canvas** рҹҺЁ вҖ” Story: pixels, sprites, real color. Learn: graphics, images,
layout. Game: a visual game board. Cast: **Pycasso** (headline role).

**Ch 6 вҖ” The Sound of Adventure** вҖ” Story: the world makes noise and moves. Learn: audio,
animation, feedback. Game: sound & motion. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *assets.*

**Ch 7 вҖ” The State Machine** вҖ” Story: menu вҶ’ play вҶ’ win/lose, cleanly. Learn: managing game
state. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *state management.*

**Ch 8 вҖ” Packing the Ship** вҖ” Story: getting the game ready to leave home. Learn: packaging,
dependencies. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *dependencies / build.*

**Ch 9 вҖ” The Lighthouse** вҖ” Story: a light others can find. Learn: deployment, hosting.
Game: **a shareable URL.** рҹҸ—пёҸ: *deployment.*

**Ch 10 вҖ” The README for Strangers** вҖ” Story: someone you'll never meet wants to play. Learn:
writing docs a stranger can follow. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *documentation.*

**Ch 11 вҖ” Demo Night** вҖ” Story: you show the family what you built. Learn: presenting with
confidence. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *demo / presentation.*

**Ch 12 вҖ” Treasure Quest v3** *(capstone / donut)* вҖ” the **published** game. Achievement: **Game Publisher** рҹҢҚ.

# рҹ“• VOLUME IV вҖ” The Infinite Guild
**Python:** AI APIs, prompt engineering, agents, automation, Git/GitHub, collaboration, ethics.
**Deliverable:** *Treasure Quest AI* вҖ” **plus the confidence to build original things.**
**Rung:** the room вҖ” propose solutions, collaborate, belong among real Builders.

**Ch 1 вҖ” A New Kind of Crew** вҖ” Story: a strange new mate joins вҖ” brilliant, but not always

**Ch 2 вҖ” The Talking Parrot** рҹҰң вҖ” Story: an NPC that truly talks. Learn: your first AI API

**Ch 3 вҖ” The Art of Asking** вҖ” Story: ask badly, get nonsense; ask well, get magic. Learn:
prompt engineering вҖ” clarity, examples, verification. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *prompt engineering.*

**Ch 4 вҖ” The AI Quartermaster** вҖ” Story: endless quests, maps, and dialogue. Learn: injecting
AI-generated content into the game. Game: **infinite quests.** Cast: Captain Byte over-trusts the AI...

**Ch 5 вҖ” The Honest Duck** рҹҰҶ вҖ” Story: ...and it makes something up. Learn: hallucination &
verification вҖ” never trust blindly. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *testing AI output.* Cast: **Professor Quackers** (check your work).

**Ch 6 вҖ” The Companion** вҖ” Story: a helper that gives hints, not answers. Learn: build an AI

**Ch 7 вҖ” Agents & Automation** вҖ” Story: teaching the crew to do chores themselves. Learn:

**Ch 8 вҖ” The Guild Repository** вҖ” Story: the whole Guild works on one codebase. Learn: Git &

**Ch 9 вҖ” Working With Others** вҖ” Story: two Builders, one game, zero chaos. Learn: issues,
reviews, teamwork; speaking like an engineer/PM. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *collaboration.*

**Ch 10 вҖ” Ethics of the Builder** вҖ” Story: with power comes *"make it kind."* Learn:
responsibility, bias, privacy, safety. рҹҸ—пёҸ: *responsible AI.* Cast: Dragon Debug (the creed completes).

**Ch 11 вҖ” The Real Problem** вӯҗ вҖ” Story: a real need вҖ” at home, at school, in the world. Learn:
propose and build a genuine solution; pitch it with confidence. рҹҸ—пёҸ: **proposing a solution / the pitch.**
*(This is the series' ultimate goal, realized.)*

**Ch 12 вҖ” Treasure Quest AI & The Infinite Guild** *(capstone / donut)* вҖ” the AI-powered game
ships, and the Builder is ready to build things no book planned. Achievement: **Guild Builder** рҹҸӣпёҸ вҖ” *you belong in the room.*
